## Filing Portal
- URL: https://bsd.sos.mo.gov
- Fee: $50 (online) / $105 (paper)
- Form: LLC-1 (Articles of Organization)
- Statute: RSMo Chapter 347

## Privacy Note
Under RSMo Section 347.039, member names and ownership percentages
are NOT disclosed in public filings. Only the organizer name and
registered agent appear in public records. The organizer is NOT
required to be a member, manager, or owner.

## FILING SEQUENCE

2. Secure Missouri registered agent
3. File Articles of Organization online ($50)
4. Receive Certificate of Organization
5. Obtain EIN from IRS (free, online at irs.gov)
6. Draft Operating Agreement (private, not filed)
7. Use this LLC as the member of the Florida LLC

## POST-FILING REQUIREMENTS
- Missouri Annual Report: Not required for LLCs (advantage over Wyoming)
- Missouri has NO state income tax on LLCs that do not conduct business in Missouri
- Federal tax: pass-through to the sole member (the Philosopher)
