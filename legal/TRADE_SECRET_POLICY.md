# AVALIA CONSULTING LLC (dba Avli Cloud)
# FL Doc L19000154273 — EIN 84-2183945
# Effective Date: June 11, 2019 (date of LLC formation)

## 1. Scope
This policy applies to all proprietary information, trade secrets,
and confidential materials of AVALIA CONSULTING LLC, dba Avli Cloud
("Company").

## 2. Designation
The following categories of information are designated as trade
secrets under the Defend Trade Secrets Act (18 U.S.C. Section
1836) and the Florida Uniform Trade Secrets Act (F.S. 688.001):

- Source code, algorithms, and software architecture
- The L7 WAY operating system and all subsystems
- The Prima programming language, compiler, and sigil system
- The 12+1 Dimensional coordinate system
- The Transmutation Engine specifications
- Business strategies, product roadmaps, and pricing models
- Patent applications and provisional filings
- Internal documentation, architecture specifications
- Customer lists, investor communications, financial projections
- Authentication systems, security protocols, encryption keys
- The Necropolis Intelligence Service (NIS) signatures and methods

## 3. Reasonable Measures
The Company maintains the following measures to protect its
trade secrets:

- Biometric access control (multi-factor: fingerprint, voice,
  face, passphrase — 3 of 4 required)
- Encrypted storage at rest (AES-256 or equivalent)
- Encrypted vault with Touch ID (macOS Keychain integration)
- All critical files gitignored from public repositories
- 3-of-3 Shamir Secret Sharing for catastrophic recovery backups
- SHA-256 hash chain for document provenance and tamper detection
- MutationObserver-based runtime injection prevention
- Rate limiting and behavioral analysis on all inputs
- Confidentiality obligations for any party granted access

## 4. Access
Access to trade secrets is limited to:
- The Founder (Alberto Valido Delgado) — full access
- The Advisory Council — access limited to their operational scope
- Legal counsel — under attorney-client privilege
- No other party has access without a signed NDA containing
  the DTSA whistleblower notice

## 5. DTSA Whistleblower Notice
Pursuant to 18 U.S.C. Section 1833(b): An individual shall not
be held criminally or civilly liable under any Federal or State
trade secret law for the disclosure of a trade secret that is
made in confidence to a government official or attorney solely
for the purpose of reporting or investigating a suspected
violation of law, or is made in a sealed court filing.

## 6. Enforcement
Violations of this policy may result in civil action under the
DTSA (federal court) and/or the Florida UTSA (state court),
seeking injunctive relief, damages, and attorney's fees.
