### Verdict: CONDITIONAL APPROVAL — two issues

**Issue 1: Registered Agent Consent (BLOCKING)**

The filing includes `ra_signature: Rodrigo L Saavedra Jr` in the
registered agent signature field. Florida law (F.S. 831.06) makes
filing a false signature forgery — a third-degree felony.

Before this form is submitted, the Philosopher must have **explicit
written or verbal confirmation** from Rodrigo L. Saavedra, Jr. that
he agrees to serve as registered agent for Avli Cloud LLC.

If he has not yet agreed: DO NOT SUBMIT. Contact him first.
If he has agreed: Proceed, but document the consent (email, text,
or written letter) and store it in .vault.

**Issue 2: Name Exposure (ADVISORY)**

The Philosopher's legal name "Alberto Valido Delgado" appears in
three places:
- Correspondence name
- Electronic signature
- Manager (MGR) name

The correspondence and signature are unavoidable — someone must sign.
The manager position, however, could use an entity name instead of
a personal name. If the Missouri LLC existed, it would replace the
personal name entirely.

**Recommendation**: File as-is with personal name. Amend within 30
days once Missouri LLC is formed. The window of personal name
exposure is brief and the address is already shielded.

**Issue 3: Principal Address = RA Address (ADVISORY)**

Using the RA's office as principal address is legal and common.
However, the Florida Division of Corporations may send physical
mail to this address. Ensure Saavedra's office will forward any
mail received for Avli Cloud LLC to the Philosopher.

### Verdict: APPROVAL — with recommendations

**Legal Integrity Assessment**

The filing is legally sound. All required fields are populated.
The purpose clause is appropriately broad. The structure is
standard for a single-member manager-managed FL LLC.

**Recommendations:**

1. **Execute the Operating Agreement on the same day as filing.**
   The Agreement (already drafted at legal/OPERATING_AGREEMENT.md)
   establishes the internal governance, IP assignments, and
   Founder's reserved powers. Without it, the LLC exists as a
   legal shell with no documented internal structure. Fill in the
   bracketed fields:
   - [MISSOURI LLC NAME] → leave as placeholder until MO is formed
   - [ADDRESS] → Saavedra's office address
   - [REGISTERED AGENT] → Rodrigo L. Saavedra, Jr.
   - [DATE OF LLC FORMATION] → the filing date from Sunbiz

2. **Obtain the EIN immediately after filing.** The IRS online
   application (irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/
   apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online) is free
   and provides the EIN instantly. The LLC needs its own EIN — it
   cannot use the Philosopher's SSN for business banking.

3. **Trade Secret Policy effective date.** Update
   legal/TRADE_SECRET_POLICY.md line 3: replace
   `[DATE OF LLC FORMATION]` with the actual filing date once
   confirmed.

4. **Copyright registration.** Once the LLC exists, file copyright
   registration for the L7 WAY system with the US Copyright Office.
   The copyright exists at creation, but registration enables
   statutory damages ($150,000 per work) and attorney's fees in
   infringement suits. Cost: $65 online.
