# ◆ FOREIGN — The Works of Others

**RIGHT COLUMN — What belongs to its creator.**

Everything in this directory was made by someone else. We respect it. We do not claim it. We do not confuse it with our work. We keep only the image memory — a record of what was witnessed, rendered through Empire's tools, but carrying the face of its original creator.

Items here:
- Belong to their original creators under their own terms
- Are not enhanced, altered, or improved by Empire
- Live outside Empire proper, gated and containerized
- Cannot cause harm or trigger action within the system
- Are labeled with their creator's name (when known) or marked as unknown
- Will never be moved LEFT — foreign remains foreign unless fully transmuted through the Forge

**To Caesar what is Caesar's. We like foreigners. This is not discrimination. It is differentiation and understanding.**
