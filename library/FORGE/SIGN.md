# ☿ THE FORGE — Purgatory of Transmutation

**MIDDLE COLUMN — The space between.**

Everything in this directory is in transition. It was inspired by something foreign, touched by the Founder's hand, but not yet sufficiently transmuted to bear the name of Empire. Or it is a work in progress that has not yet been completed. Or it is a memory of an attempt that may or may not continue.

Items here:
- Have been examined, deconstructed, learned from
- May contain both foreign elements and original creative spark
- Are accessible for re-definition and understanding
- May eventually move LEFT (to Empire, if the creative spark completes the transmutation)
- May remain here forever (not everything finishes, and that is acceptable)
- Will never move RIGHT (what was once touched by the Forge does not become more foreign)

**Nuance lives here. Not everything is binary. Sufficient change is relative to each product.**
