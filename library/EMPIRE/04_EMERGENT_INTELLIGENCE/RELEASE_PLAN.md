## Submission Strategy

### Primary Target: AI / Cognitive Science Journals
1. **Artificial Intelligence (Elsevier)**
   - Premier journal for foundational AI research
   - Impact Factor: ~14.0
   - Rolling submissions, ~6 month review

2. **Neural Computation (MIT Press)**
   - Computational neuroscience and cognitive architectures
   - Ideal for the staged transformation + emergence argument

3. **Cognitive Science (Wiley)**
   - Interdisciplinary cognitive science
   - Good fit for the biological inspiration angle

### Secondary Target: Conferences
4. **NeurIPS (Neural Information Processing Systems)**
   - Workshop paper or main track
   - Deadline: Typically May for December conference

5. **AAAI (Association for the Advancement of AI)**
   - Broad AI audience
   - Deadline: Typically August for February conference

6. **CogSci (Annual Meeting of the Cognitive Science Society)**
   - Best for the interdisciplinary framing

### Pre-Submission Steps
1. Complete experimental validation section with benchmarks
2. Expand LTA-7 Python implementation with reproducibility package
3. Format in journal-specific LaTeX template
4. Post preprint to arXiv (cs.AI or cs.NE)
5. File provisional patent if applicable ($320)
