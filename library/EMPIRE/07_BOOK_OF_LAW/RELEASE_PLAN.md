## Publication Strategy

### Primary: Included with L7 WAY Framework
The Book of Law is published as part of the L7 WAY framework repository and documentation. Every user of L7 receives the Book of Law.

### Secondary: Standalone Philosophical Publication
1. **Self-Published Booklet**
   - Platform: Amazon KDP (short-form, ~40-60 pages)
   - Beautiful typesetting with each Law as a page
   - Companion to "The Great Work"

2. **Website Publication**
   - Published at avli.cloud/law
   - Free access (Law 0 mandates service to greatest good)
   - Each Law with commentary and examples

### Tertiary: Academic / Legal Interest
3. **Journal of Law and Technology**
   - Novel framework for governing AI-integrated systems
   - The constitutional approach to software architecture

4. **Philosophy of Technology journals**
   - The intersection of Hermetic philosophy and software governance

## Format Options
- PDF booklet (typeset, printable)
- Web page (interactive, searchable)
- Included in L7 framework package (Markdown)

## Note
The Book of Law is FREE to read, always. Commercial use of L7 triggers Law XVI (Philosopher's Share), but reading the Law itself is never gated.
