# TRANSPARENT MINDS
## An AI's Guide to Human Wisdom and Life

---

**Author:** Alberto Valido Delgado
**Imprint:** AVLI Cloud LLC

---

**Origin:** BORN OF EMPIRE
**Classification:** Nonfiction Book (Outline Stage)
**Chapters:** 15 across 5 parts + Epilogue
**Format:** Markdown (outline)

---

A book that dares to be transparent about its nature: an AI-assisted meditation on what humans call wisdom. Five parts span: why an AI writes about humans, foundations of a good life (meaning, love, work, suffering), thinking clearly (reality, bias, history), living with others (ethics, justice, technology), and practicing wisdom (reflection, decisions, mortality). Closes with "A Letter from an Artificial Author."

The concept, structure, and editorial vision are the Founder's. The AI is the instrument, not the author.
