Prima is a graph-centric programming language rooted in Kabbalah, Alchemy, Astrology, and Tarot. Its 22 operations map to the 22 Hebrew letters and the 22 Major Arcana of the Tarot. Programs do not compile into bytecode or machine instructions — they compile into sigils: weighted hypergraphs where meaning lives in the edges, not the nodes.

**22 Operations:** Each mapped to a Hebrew letter and Tarot archetype.
**5 Human Verbs:** Summon, Transmute, Connect, Share/Seal, Remember/Forget.
**Compilation Target:** Weighted hypergraphs (sigils) — not generic symbols, but graphs where every edge carries 12-dimensional weights.

The language embodies Law XLV: the Forge speaks Prima, and Prima speaks through the Forge.
