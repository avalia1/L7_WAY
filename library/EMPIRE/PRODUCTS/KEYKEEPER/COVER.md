Keykeeper is the credential management system of the L7 empire. It enforces Law XXX: biometrics only, NO passwords. Every credential access requires Touch ID authentication and is verified against the machine's hardware UUID.

**Core Principles:**
- Biometrics only — no passwords ever stored or transmitted
- Touch ID encrypted — credentials locked to the biological owner
- Machine UUID verified — credentials locked to the physical hardware
- Gateway-mediated — all access passes through the Gateway, logged and auditable
- Rotation windows — credentials automatically rotate on configurable schedules

Keykeeper does not merely store secrets. It guards them the way a castle guard guards the gate: with identity verification, physical presence confirmation, and a complete audit trail of every entry and exit.
