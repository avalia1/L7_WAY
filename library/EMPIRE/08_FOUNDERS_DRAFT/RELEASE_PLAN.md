## Publication Strategy

### Primary: Internal / Archival
The Founder's Draft is the sovereign document of the Empire. Its primary purpose is internal governance, not public distribution. However, sealed versions serve as legal proof of authorship and priority.

### Secondary: Legal / IP Filing
- Submitted as exhibit in copyright registration
- Submitted as exhibit in patent filings
- Submitted as exhibit in any IP dispute
- Referenced in all licensing agreements

### Tertiary: Future Historical Publication
When L7 WAY achieves significant adoption, the Founder's Draft may be published as a historical document — the "Federalist Papers" of the system.

- **Format:** Annotated edition with commentary
- **Publisher:** Self-published or university press
- **Audience:** Historians of technology, legal scholars, system architects

## Access Control
- Full text: Founder only (Law XV)
- Sealed versions: Available for legal proceedings
- Excerpts: Published through Book of Law and Architecture documents
