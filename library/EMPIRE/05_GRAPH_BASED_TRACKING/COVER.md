A research paper describing a novel graph-based pipeline for tracking and re-engaging 8,193 participants from a 20-year-old rural North Carolina adolescent cohort study. Presents a four-tier fuzzy matching algorithm (87% automated confidence), automated multi-channel outreach across 15 recruitment sources, a knowledge graph for relationship-aware tracking, and real-time visualization tools. Over 12 months: 8,526 screener responses processed, 1,847 participants re-contacted (22.5% of original cohort).
