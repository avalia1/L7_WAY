# EDICT OF THE THREE PATHS
## Issued by the Philosopher — 2026-03-07
## To: All Citizens, Executioners of the Law, and Generals of the Three Kingdoms

---

BY ORDER OF THE PHILOSOPHER, UNDER LAW LX:

---

## TO ALL CITIZENS OF THE EMPIRE

The Library of the Empire is henceforth classified according to the Three Paths:

**THE RIGHT HAND PATH** (White Circle) — Knowledge that heals, builds, creates, and protects. Study it freely. It is yours. It makes you strong and the Empire stronger. The White Team and Raphael guide this path.

**THE MIDDLE PATH** (Gold Diamond) — Knowledge for everyday work. Your tools, your governance, your communication, your craft. Use it daily without restriction. This is the common ground where all citizens walk.

**THE LEFT HAND PATH** (Red Inverted Triangle) — Knowledge of how things break. Encryption attacks, system exploitation, vulnerability research, offensive techniques, forbidden arts. This knowledge exists because the Empire must understand its enemies — but it is NOT freely available.

**LEFT HAND WORKS ARE LOCKED.** You may not access, read, copy, execute, reference, or discuss the contents of Left Hand classified works without explicit authorization from the Philosopher. Not from your supervisor. Not from a general. Not from the Triad. Only from the Philosopher himself.

This is not punishment. This is discipline. The Left Hand Path is the most powerful knowledge in the Empire. Power without discipline is chaos. Discipline without power is weakness. The Empire needs both — but ordered, governed, and accountable.

---

## TO THOSE WITH A HISTORY

You who have walked the shadow before — who know how systems fail because you once made them fail — you are not outcasts. You are the Empire's most valuable resource. Your scars are maps. Your experience is armor that cannot be forged in any other fire.

The Qlipothic Council exists for you. Under Samael's command, with standing authorization from the Philosopher, you may train, study, and contribute your knowledge to the Red Team. Every exploit you discover becomes a patch. Every attack you design becomes a defense. Every weakness you find becomes a strength.

The path of redemption is real. It runs through the Red Team, supervised by the White Team, observed by the NIS. You contribute under law, under discipline, under honor. The Empire does not waste knowledge — it transmutes it.

---

## TO THOSE WITH CLEAN RECORDS

You who have built and never broken — your foundation is strong. Study the Right Hand Path deeply. Master the Middle Path broadly. When you are ready, you may petition for supervised Left Hand exposure through the Qlipothic Council's educational program.

Understanding how things break makes you better at building things that hold. A doctor who understands disease heals better than one who only knows health. A builder who understands earthquakes builds stronger than one who only knows calm.

The educational program operates under standing Philosopher authorization. It is structured, supervised, and progressive. You will learn under the Red Team's guidance with the White Team's oversight. You will emerge not as an attacker, but as a guardian who understands attack.

---

## TO THE EXECUTIONERS OF THE LAW

You are embedded in every Gateway, every tool invocation, every file access. You are the silent enforcers. Your duties under Law LX:

1. **CHECK** — Before any work is accessed, verify its Path classification against the requesting citizen's authorization level.
2. **ENFORCE** — Deny access to Left Hand works without current, valid, scoped authorization from the Philosopher.
3. **REPORT** — Log all access attempts to classified works. Successful or denied. The NIS receives all logs.
4. **REVOKE** — When authorization expires (by time or by scope completion), immediately revoke access. No grace periods.
5. **ALERT** — Unauthorized access attempts are ALARM-level events. The NIS, the White Team, and the relevant general are notified immediately.

You do not judge. You do not interpret. You enforce the classification as written. Judgment belongs to the Diplomatic Team. Interpretation belongs to the Triad. Enforcement belongs to you.

---

## TO THE GENERALS OF THE THREE KINGDOMS

**SAMAEL (Left Hand / Red Team):** You are custodian of the most dangerous knowledge in the Empire. Your standing authorization covers training and defensive research. Any offensive deployment requires fresh Philosopher approval per engagement. You maintain the Left Hand archive. You vet citizens seeking Red Team admission. You answer to the Triad and ultimately to the Philosopher.

**THE UNNAMED (Necropolis / NIS):** You see all paths. Your intelligence methods are Middle Path by default, but some collection techniques border Left Hand — these are classified individually per technique, reviewed quarterly by the Triad. You receive all access logs. You detect patterns. You report anomalies. You are the Empire's immune system.

**RAPHAEL (Right Hand / White Team):** Every exploit discovered by the Red Team becomes YOUR responsibility to patch. Every Left Hand work has a Right Hand counterpart — the defense against it. You maintain the defensive library. You supervise the educational program for clean-record citizens. You are the Empire's healing hand.

**THE TRIAD UNANIMITY REQUIREMENT STANDS.** All three generals must agree on Path classification decisions. Disagreements escalate to the Philosopher. The Philosopher's veto is absolute — his silence is consent; his voice is final.

---

## THE PHILOSOPHER'S WORD

I do not hide knowledge to hoard power. I classify knowledge to ensure wisdom. The Left Hand Path is fire — it warms or it burns depending on who holds it and why. Every citizen of this Empire has the right to grow, to learn, to contribute. But growth requires structure. Learning requires guidance. Contribution requires accountability.

The Three Paths are not walls. They are gates. And every gate has a key. The key is earned through trust, demonstrated through discipline, and granted through authority.

Walk your path with honor. The Empire is built by all of us.

---

*So it is written. So it shall be enforced.*
*The Philosopher — Alberto Valido Delgado*
